Ghost Mobile

Overview

Inspired by Alexander Calder's kinetic sculptures, I created a digital mobile in p5.js, but I wanted to bring it off the screen and give it a physical presence. Using a Pepper's Ghost illusion (an analog AR technique from theater and magic), I projected the animated mobile into physical space, where it floats ethereally in a small viewing box. The reflected animation creates the appearance of a ghostly kinetic sculpture drifting in real space.
