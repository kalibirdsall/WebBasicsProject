Kaleidoscope Camera

Overview

I created an interactive p5.js photobooth that uses the webcam on the viewer's computer to capture their image in real-time and transforms it with a kaleidoscopic effect.

To try it, out click this link. Note that you'll have to give it permission to use your webcam for it to work on your computer. I hope you try it out!
